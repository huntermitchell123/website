Face Prediction Project

Fully developed by Hunter Mitchell



Both models are trained with 3000 images of faces from the imdb-wiki dataset. The gender model is a simple classification model and the age model is a bit more complicated regression model. They both use the widely popular convolutional neural network DenseNet. The gender prediction model has an accuracy of about 92%, while the age prediction model has a mean absolute error of less than 8. Both models work better when you are closer to the camera and showing your full face and hair. Test out what works and what doesn't and feel free to let me know! I look forward to implementing more to this interface soon!
